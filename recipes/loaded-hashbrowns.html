Loaded Hashbrowns

Description

A classic cheese and egg scramble with a slice of turkey bacon, topped above an oven baked Cavendish Farms potato hashbrown. Can't go wrong!

Ingredients for 2 Servings

  • 4 Cavendish potato Hashbrowns
  • 4 Slices turkey bacon
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/3 C cheddar cheeese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter
  • Scallions for garnish

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 350F. Place hashbrowns on a baking sheet. Once oven warm, place in oven for 15 minutes
  2. Scramble eggs and add cheese to scramble. Add a pinch of salt and pepper
  3. When 15 minutes has elapsed, flip the hashbrowns and return to the oven for another 15 minutes
  4. Start cooking turkey bacon
  5. When there's about 7 minutes left on the oven, place small skillet on high with 1 tbsp of cooking oil
  6. Test pan heat with a bit of egg. If it turns white immediately, it's the right temperature. Add scrambled egg and cheese mixture to the hot pan and immediately sift it around using a rubber spatula, ensuring all of the egg mixture cooks. Remove from heat and set aside
  7. Once all things are cooked, assemble hashbrowns on a plate. Place folded turkey bacon strip on each hashbrown, top with the egg scramble and scallions for garnish. Serve with buttered toast and fresh tomato slices
